Notation

Notation is a desktop application for music creation with accessability in mind. Inspired by my own experiences with music production, I wanted to create a tool that would allow me to create music easily without needing to be familiar with large and cumbersome composition programs or well versed in tradition notation.

How it works

Under the hood, Notation extends and adds functionality a rust library called rust-music-theory to create an object-oriented way to interface with music programatically. The command line prototype generates a custom musical language that is then parsed and interpreted by the program. This allows for a simple and intuitive way to create music without needing to know how to read music notation by naming notes and chords in a way that is familiar to anyone who has ever played an instrument.
